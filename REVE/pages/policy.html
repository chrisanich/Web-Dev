Our Privacy Policy

Cookies

Cookies are small files which are stored on a user's computer. They are designed to hold a modest amount of data specific to a particular client and website, and can be accessed either by the web server or the client computer. We use various third party cookies on Label and Artist websites, buylinks and mailouts in order to build a picture of our audiences and tailor communications to them. If you wish to remove cookies from your browser, you are free to do so via your browser's settings at any time.

Marketing

Rêve Discos may share your Data with trusted third parties and digital marketing tools in order to target banners and ads to you when you visit other websites. In such cases, we will do our best to protect your Data and prevent it being retained by third parties.
