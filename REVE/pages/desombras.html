De Sombras

Y Siluetas

Pop and Latin Music

This music band, made up of Camila Salgado (vocals), Sebastián Roque (keyboards), Alamiro Arias (guitar), Gabriel Rivas (drums) and Fernando Fuentes (bass), stands out for its four years of experience, which include tours throughout America and Europe, and for also having two released albums. They have made a difference on the national scene, addressing contingent issues that reveal the current situation of Chile as a country.

Rêve Discos

Chilean Independent

Record Label

Rêve Discos is a Chilean independent record label working from South America to the world. Its chief music genres are rock, pop and electronic music. Although most of its bands are from Chile, international artists work in the catalogue.
