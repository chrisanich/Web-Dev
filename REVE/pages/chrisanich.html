Chris Anich

Rock, pop and electronic

music producer

Chilean musician and director of Rêve Discos, who published in 2018 an EP sung in Spanish called "Tomar el control (Take on)", with which he has made three tours to Mexico. In June 2020, he released an instrumental titled "Bienvenidos a la Tierra (Welcome to Earth)" and is preparing to release an upcoming single and a casual video game featuring his music in the coming weeks. On stage, he performs in a band format, singing and playing the electronic section of his songs, along with a guitarist, a bassist and a drummer who accompany him.

Feria Pulsar

Santiago, Chile

Showcase broadcast on November 18, 2020, from the SCD Bellavista Room, Santiago, Chile, within the framework of the tenth edition of the Pulsar Fair, held for the first time in 100% digital format.
