Rêve Discos

Chilean Independent

Record Label

Rêve Discos is a Chilean independent record label working from South America to the world. Its chief music genres are rock, pop and electronic music. Although most of its bands are from Chile, international artists will be soon in our catalogue.

Our music

Rock, pop and electronic

music

Our mission is to produce, distribute and publish the music of our artists so that according to their talent, they have the opportunity to show their full potential to their audience. Give them a look in this music player and then go to their websites to know more about them. Listen to their music directly from their platforms to support them. You never know what day your favorite artist arises.
